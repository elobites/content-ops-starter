Do you ever feel a sudden burst of energy after a snack, only to hit a "wall" 30 minutes later? You’re likely experiencing a blood sugar spike.

### Post Highlights:

*   GI Explained: A simple breakdown of how different carbs affect your blood glucose.

*   The Rollercoaster Effect: Why high-GI snacks (like sugary crackers or white bread) leave you feeling tired and hungry again almost immediately.

*   Smart Fueling: Why choosing low-GI ingredients (like nuts, seeds, and whole grains) provides sustained energy.

*   The Elobites Advantage: Our snacks are designed to keep your glucose stable, so you can stay focused until dinner.
